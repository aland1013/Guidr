The #1 App for Backcountry Guides

Log trips

Whether you are a professional backcountry guide with years of experience or you are just getting started, Guidr is the app for you. Once you have created an account, you can log all of your adventures by date, location, distance, duration, and type. With Guidr, browse a list of all of your previous trips or pull up the details of a particular tip with the click of a button.

Upload pics

Guidr offers our users free, unlimited, full-resolution online photo storage! Just upload your photos, tag them to link them to a particular one of your trips, and then safely delete them from your phone or camera to free up space on your device. Easily access your photos on the go with our app.

Build resume

If you lead backcountry trips professionally or aspire to, you can use Guidr to showcase your outdoor experience to potential employers. With our resume feature, you can export a PDF complete with all of your previous backcountry experience, and any licenses, organizations, and other affiliations included in your profile.
